The Problem

92% of American teenageers use social media, but 40% feel they can't express themselves online.

Social media platforms today are all about parties and videos of funny cats... but what about the teenagers who want to share a deeper story of who they really are?

Our Solution

Our team is building a platform to help teenagers share a more authentic story, with a focus on passions, ideas, accomplishments and dreams.

We believe the platform will not only help teens express themselves, but might also help them impress colleges and potential employers. Think of this as a more personal "LinkedIn for teenagers" - giving us a platform that encouarges professional and thoughtful web identity.
